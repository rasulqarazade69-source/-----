Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, is a professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. He began his senior club career playing for Sporting CP before signing with Manchester United in 2003, where he developed into a formidable forward. In 2009, he transferred to Real Madrid for a then-world record transfer fee, where he became the club's all-time top scorer. In 2018, he moved to Juventus and later returned to Manchester United in 2021. Ronaldo has won numerous awards throughout his career, including five Ballon d'Or titles, and has set multiple records for goals scored in both club and international competitions.
